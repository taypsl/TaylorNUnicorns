Once upon a time, a girl named Taylor traveled to a Far Away Place.  It was only supposed to be a short visit.

At first glance, it seemed like the Far Away Place was kind of Boring.  But on the inside, the people she met were very Not Boring.

After a little time had passed, Taylor started to notice something a bit different about two people in particular.

It seemed like Evan and Chienlan were...

Unicorns!

Just as Taylor decided to stay in the Far Away Place a little longer with Unicorn Evan and Unicorn Chienlan, a new person arrived.

Pretty soon, Taylor began to notice something different about this new friend, too...

She was also a unicorn!

Taylor loved making magical things with the team of friendly unicorns.

It was so much fun that everyone agreed they should invite more people to help them make more magical things.

That's when Allison sailed by!

And after only a little time in the Far Away Place, it was obvious to everyone...

That Allison, too, was a unicorn!

Then Elaine came to stay!

At first, she didn't look like a unicorn.  But everyone knows unicorns are hard to disguise, especially if you know how to look for one.

And sure enough...

Unicorn!

The team of unicorns worked hard to make wonderfully magical things, and Taylor loved every minute they spent together.  It truly was both Good and Great.

But too, too soon the day became very late in the Far Away Place.  Taylor knew the time was coming when she'd have to go home and leave her friendly unicorns for a while.

And she knew she would miss them a lot.

But as Taylor started out on her long journey home, she noticed something had changed...

... and she was very grateful for her unicorn friends.

The End
